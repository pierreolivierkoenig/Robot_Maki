Dynamixel Motor Record and Playback Motion (Synchronous Write).vi

 Save

Saves the motor list, step times, positions, and speed scale to file.

Torque on

Turn on the torque for each motor.

Initialize or Go

Slowly moves all of the motors to the positions at Frame.

Frame

The current frame in the sequence. This corresponds to the sets of positions the motors will move to when you click Go. This also corresponds to where playback starts. This is also the frame that will be deleted when you click Delete. Finally, this is the frame that Insert will insert recorded positions and where Paste will insert copied positions.

Stop Playback

Stops playback of the motion.

Delay Time (ms)

Time delay added after each sequence frame before the next frame is executed.

Speed Scale (%)

Speeds up or slows down the entire playback of the motion. The step times used for play back are calculated as Step Times / Speed Scale.

Play

Starts playback of the motion from Frame assuming that the motors are currently at Frame. To be sure that the motors are in the correct initial position, hit Go before Play.

Delete

Deletes the sequence frame at Frame

Insert

Same as record except Insert will insert the recorded positions at Frame.

Record

Records the current positions of all of the motors and adds the data as a frame to the end of the sequence on the table. Step Time is also recorded in the table.

Display Table

Displays the motor list, step times, and positions.

Step Time (ms)

How long it will take for the motors to move from the frame being recorded to the next frame.

Load

Loads saved work, which includes: motor list, step times, positions, and speed scale.

Quit

Stops the VI.

VISA resource name

Specifies which port to use. Serial ports that are powered on and connected to your computer are visible in the VISA resource name pull-down menu. Select the resource name from the VISA resource name pull-down menu. If your port does not appear in this list, enter the instrument descriptor using the following format:

Interface Type[board index]::Address::INSTR.

Examples:

Serial: COM1

Refer to the LabVIEW Help for more information about specifying a VISA resource name.

Refresh Motor List

Scans for connected motors starting at ID Starting From and ending at Up to.

Torque off

Turn off the torque for each motor.

Motor Torques

List of motors found. You can turn individual motor torques on or off.

    		Cluster

      		On

      		Off

      		ID

From

The first ID of a motor the program will search for.

Up to

The last ID of a motor the program will search for.

Baud rate (57600 bps)

Known or desired baud rate of the motors.

Copy

Copies the sequence frames selected on the table.

Paste

Pastes and inserts the copied sequence frames at Frame.
